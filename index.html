B.U.C.S

KNOW YOUR FUTURE CAMPUS.

Bicol University's College of Science, established in 2004, arose from the merger of three distinct programs: Biology, Chemistry, and Computer Science. This strategic union aimed to consolidate resources and expertise, fostering a robust hub for scientific advancements in Bicol. Today, the college embraces its four-fold mission of providing quality education, conducting impactful research, offering community-focused extension services, and generating sustainable income. Students delve into diverse fields, deciphering life's mysteries in biology labs, crafting technological solutions in computer science workshops, and exploring the secrets of the natural world through chemistry experiments. Graduates emerge equipped to tackle regional challenges, advocate for sustainability, and shape the future of Bicol's scientific landscape. The college's story is one of collaboration, innovation, and a unwavering commitment to serving the region through the power of science
